# Engineering Modernization

AI has lowered the cost of writing code. It has not lowered the cost of operating bad systems. This repo is one place for the engineering team to understand what that means and what we are going to do about it.

---

## What is in here

**[MANIFESTO.md](./MANIFESTO.md)**
The beliefs that guide how we work: what we prioritize, what we refuse to tolerate, and what modernization actually means for this team. Start here if you are new or want to understand the thinking behind our decisions.

**[docs/industry-data.md](./docs/industry-data.md)**
Curated research from the 2025-2026 industry landscape, organized by theme. Useful if you want the data behind the argument, or if you need to make the case for investment in delivery infrastructure. Every stat has a source.

**[docs/tooling.md](./docs/tooling.md)**
How to think about tooling in the AI era, what our delivery system needs to be capable of, and what we should build or adopt to get there. Useful for tech leads and anyone thinking about platform investment.

---

This is a living reference, not a policy document. If something is wrong or outdated, open a PR.
